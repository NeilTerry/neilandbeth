Linville Ridge Country Club

Linville Ridge Country Club is located in Linville, NC, in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains. The club sits adjacent to both Grandfather and Sugar mountains and is approximately 15 minutes from Boone, NC. Its location makes it easily accessible by car.
