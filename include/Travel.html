Travel

Travel to Linville Ridge will likely require a combination of air and car travel if you do not live close enough to drive. Once in Linville, shuttles will be provided to and from the wedding events from a limited number of locations, but a personal vehicle will be neccesary to get around otherwise.

Linville Ridge Country Club

Linville Ridge Country Club (golden peak on map) is located in Linville, NC, in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains. The club sits adjacent to both Grandfather and Sugar mountains and is approximately 15 minutes from Boone, NC. Its location makes it easily accessible by car.

Air Travel

Linville Ridge is accessible from a number of regional airports, listed below with approximate travel times via car.

  • Charlotte Douglas (CLT) - 1 hour, 50 minutes
  • Raleigh-Durham (RDU) - 3 hours, 10 minutes
  • Piedmont Triad (PTI) - 2 hours, 10 minutes
  • Atlanta Hartsfield-Jackson (ATL) - 5 hours
  • Asheville Regional (AVL) - 1 hour, 10 minutes
  • Hickory Regional (HKY) - 1 hour, 20 minutes
  • Tri Cities TN/VA (TRI) - 1 hour
